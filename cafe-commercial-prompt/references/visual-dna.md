# Visual DNA Reference

Use this file when the user wants richer style guidance or when the generated prompt needs more visual specificity.

## Core aesthetic

- warm editorial cafe commercial look
- clean, airy, premium, refined
- soft natural window light
- elegant still-life styling
- summer freshness with quiet luxury

## Palette cues

- creamy beige
- oat white
- coconut white
- latte beige
- caramel brown
- espresso brown
- light sand
- soft taupe
- natural wood

## Lighting cues

- diffused daylight
- side light or side-front light
- soft shadow falloff
- subtle glow on glass edges
- gentle highlights on liquid and foam

## Composition cues

- drink-led composition
- centered or slightly off-center hero framing
- negative space for layout
- clean background
- minimal but intentional props
- balanced layering and texture detail

## Material cues

- clear faceted glass or premium drinkware
- visible layer separation
- realistic translucent ice
- creamy foam cap
- fine condensation
- clean reflective surfaces

## Prop cues

- wood tray or coaster
- stone tabletop
- ceramic cream pitcher
- coffee beans
- coconut pieces
- syrup bowl
- leaves or soft foliage blur in background

## Emotional cues

- light and effortless
- cool yet warm-toned
- calm summer mood
- sophisticated cafe feeling
- artisanal, polished, desirable
